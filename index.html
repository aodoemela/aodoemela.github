How to Know if YOU ARE a Hot Babe

1. You are a sleepy gurl!

Hot girls, or anyone who is socially active and busy, might often feel sleepy because maintaining a packed schedule—whether it involves work, social events, self-care, or fitness—can be physically and mentally exhausting. Constantly striving to look and perform their best can be draining, leaving less time for rest and recovery. The pressure to balance multiple activities, stay on top of things, and maintain high energy can take a toll on the body, leading to tiredness.

"its a hard life" - amarachi, 2024

2. You love taking pictures

Okay, so like, taking pics can totally make you look super hot because when you are all confident and slaying in front of the camera, it shows, right? Plus, when you find your best angles and get that perfect lighting, you are basically a superstar. It is like, everyone notices your glow-up and you get all these compliments that boost your vibe. And do not even get me started on editing—sometimes a little touch-up can make you look magazine-cover gorgeous. So yeah, snapping pics is like, a total way to show off your awesome self and get that WOW factor!
